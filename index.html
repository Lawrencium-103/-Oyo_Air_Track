What is it?

A zero-cost digital infrastructure that synthesizes global satellite data with localized mapping to monitor Oyo's environment in real-time.

Why it matters?

Air quality is an invisible killer. By making it visible, we help reduce respiratory illnesses and build climate-resilient cities (SDG 3 & 11).

The Purpose

To bridge the data divide in low-resource settings, providing policymakers with the proof needed for sustainable urban planning.
